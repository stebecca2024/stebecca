4/27 schedule outline:

  • 9:50 A.M. | Metra to 59th Street
    • Stefan's group leaves Staypineapple for Millennium Station to catch the 10:10 A.M. Metra to Hyde Park (ME811). All are welcome to join!

  • 10:00 A.M. | early shuttle to Bond Chapel
    • Rebecca's group leaves Staypineapple on the shuttle bus to Bond Chapel. Shuttle departs from the loading zone in front of Staypineapple hotel lobby. Anyone who wants an early ride to Hyde Park is welcome to come along!

  • 11:00 A.M. | Bond Chapel shuttle 2
    • Shuttle departs from the loading zone in front of Staypineapple.

  • Noon | Wedding Ceremony
    • Where: Bond Chapel, 1010 E 59th St, Chicago, IL (CLICK TO VIEW in Google Maps)

    Guests arranging their own transportation to Bond Chapel: please arrive between 11:30 and 11:50 A.M.! Bond Chapel is located within the quad, and has no direct road access. From there, one of the ushers can guide you to the chapel entrance.

  • 12:30 P.M. | Refreshments
    • Bond Chapel courtyard & Swift Hall common room. We recommend using the bathrooms, as there aren't any on the train! Next chance will be at 3:15.

  • 1:10 P.M. & 1:20 P.M. | shuttles to 'L' train charter
    • Meet at corner of 59th St & Ellis Ave to board a quick shuttle to the Cottage Grove 'L' station, the terminus of the CTA Green Line, located on an elevated structure above E 63rd St at Cottage Grove Ave. We board the chartered 'L' train at the station.

    Shuttle 1 leaves at 1:10

    Shuttle 2 leaves at 1:20. The train leaves Cottage Grove at 1:30!

  • 1:45 P.M. | hotel shuttle (alternative to 'L' charter)

    However: transportation will NOT be provided from the hotel to the banquet hall. (From Staypineapple, there are many convenient ways to reach the dinner venue in Chinatown Square Plaza.)

  • 3:15 P.M. | Garfield Park Conservatory (bathroom break!)
    • Hop off the train at Conservatory-Central Park Dr (CLICK TO OPEN in Google Maps) 'L' station. From there, it's a ~1-minute walk to the Garfield Park Conservatory (CLICK to open website). Use the restrooms and enjoy the plants!

    After everyone gets a chance to use the bathroom, we may also sneak in a group picture or two...

  • 4:00 P.M. | Big group photo 'L' charter resumes
    • Before hopping back on the train, we'll take a photo of the entire group on the platform at Conservatory-Central Park Dr station.

  • 5:30 P.M. | Reception dinner at Ming Hin
    • Ming Hin restaurant in Chinatown Square Plaza (CLICK TO OPEN in Google Maps). 'L' charter will drop passengers at the Cermak-Chinatown station on the Red Line, a 3-minute walk from Ming Hin.

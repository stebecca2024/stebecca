Before the wedding - Friday, April 26

On the night of 4/26, we will host an informal gathering in the first floor of the hotel. Stop by and say hello! Staypineapple guests get 2 free drink tokens per-room. Additional drinks and snacks will be available at normal prices.

  • 8:30 - 10:30 P.M. (Central timezone) | Social hour
    • Where: The Atwood (restaurant in the lobby of Staypineapple)

Itinerary - Saturday, April 27

4/27 will be a busy day! The wedding cermony begins at noon, so please arrive at Bond Chapel between 11:30 A.M. and noon. After the ceremony, we will have several activities—all of which are optional. Transportation provided between activities.

4/27 schedule outline: (all times are Central timezone)

  • 10:00 A.M. | Metra to 59th Street
    • Stefan's group leaves Staypineapple for Millennium Station to catch the 10:10 Metra to Hyde Park (ME8311).

  • 11:00 A.M. | Bond Chapel shuttle
    • Shuttle boards at the loading zone in front of Staypineapple hotel lobby.

  • 11:50 A.M. - 12:30 P.M. | Wedding Ceremony
    • Where: Bond Chapel, 1010 E 59th St, Chicago, IL (CLICK TO VIEW in Google Maps) Guests arranging their own transportation to Bond Chapel: please arrive a few minutes early! Bond chapel is located within the quad, and has no direct road access. The closest access point is through the archway on 59th street, near the corner with Ellis Avenue (this is also our shuttle dropoff point). From there, one of the ushers can guide you to the chapel entrance.

  • 12:30 P.M. - 1:25 P.M. | Refreshments
    • Bond Chapel courtyard & Swift Hall common room

  • 1:15 P.M. - 1:30 P.M. | 'L' train charter begins
    • Meet at corner of 59th St & Ellis Ave to board a quick shuttle to the Cottage Grove 'L' station, the terminus of the CTA Green Line, located on an elevated structure above 63rd St at Cottage Grove Ave. At the station, we'll board a chartered 'L' train. Shuttle 1 leaves at 1:15; shuttle 2 leaves at 1:25. The train leaves Cottage Grove at 1:30!

  • 3:15 P.M. | Garfield Park Conservatory (bathroom break!)
    • Hop off the train at Conservatory-Central Park Dr (CLICK TO OPEN in Google Maps) 'L' station. From there, it's a ~1-minute walk to the Garfield Park Conservatory (CLICK to open website). Use the restrooms and enjoy the plants!

  • 4:00 P.M. | 'L' Charter resumes

  • 5:30 P.M. | Reception dinner at Ming Hin
    • Ming Hin restaurant in Chinatown Square Plaza (CLICK TO OPEN in Google Maps). 'L' Charter will drop passengers at the Cermak-Chinatown station on the Red Line, a 3-minute walk from Ming Hin.

Transportation to Bond Chapel

On the morning of 4/27, we will offer 2 options to travel from the Loop to Bond Chapel.

We recommend taking the Metra to Hyde Park. Don't worry about train tickets—we will arrange group tickets in advance.

  • Stefan will lead a group on the 2-block walk from Staypineapple to Millennium Station. CLICK TO VIEW in Google Maps.
  • Board any Metra Electric train at Millennium Station (located under the Bean!).
  • Take the train to "59th St./Univ. of Chicago".
  • After exiting the train, Bond Chapel is a 10-15-minute walk along the Midway Plaisance.

We will also offer a shuttle bus from Staypineapple. Please let us know in the RSVP form if you would prefer to travel by train or by bus.
